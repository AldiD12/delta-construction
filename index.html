§ 04 — How we work Process

Six clear stages, from first walk-through to handover keys.

01Discovery
Site visit & scope brief
A director walks the property with you and the architect. We agree what is being built, what is being kept, and what the non-negotiables are — within seven days you receive a written scope.
~ 1 weekNo charge
02Tender
Fixed-price quotation
Itemised pricing against the drawings and spec, broken down by trade and stage. Provisional sums clearly flagged. Quotation valid for 60 days.
2–3 weeksFrom £950
03Pre-start
Contract, programme & payment plan
JCT signed, party-wall and CDM regs handled. Construction programme issued with critical-path milestones. Monthly valuation schedule agreed.
2 weeksPre-construction
04Strip-out
Demolition, structure & first fix
Steels, foundations, drainage, framing, first-fix electrics & plumbing. Weekly site report with photos. Building Control inspections booked in advance.
8–14 weeks50–60% of value
05Finishes
Second fix, joinery & decoration
Plastering, screeding, joinery install, decoration, stone, tiling, sanitaryware, lighting. Weekly client walk-through.
6–10 weeks30–40% of value
06Handover
Snagging & 10-year warranty
Joint inspection with the client and architect. All defects rectified before handover, O&M manual and warranties issued. 12-month defects period begins.
2 weeksFinal 5%
